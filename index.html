Our Mission

We believe that a job isn't just about the work, it's also about the community we serve. When you hire us, you're hiring a team that is personally invested in your project. We take pride in our work, and are committed to bringing you quality results as quickly and safely as possible.

Land clearing does not have to be a one-size-fits-all solution. Sometimes, when folks hear the term "land clearing", they envision big, destructive machines, that indiscriminately tear apart their property. Yes, sometimes your project might call for a dozer, or a large piece of forestry equipment, but, often, homeowners need a more tailored approach to their clearing project.

Got a yard or other outdoor space you'd like to enjoy but can't access? This is where we come in. Bad Hog Land Clearing specializes in selective land clearing - removing select trash trees, dead material, and underbrush, giving you better visiblity, access, and letting your older, more well-established trees a chance to really shine.

Land Clearing

Land clearing is the first step for any construction project. Whether that's having fencing installed, or laying foundation for a house, you need the space to do it! We'll lay the groundwork for your project by removing any vegetation standing in the way.

Tree Removal

Once we've discussed the scope and parameters of the project with you, we'll use a combination of power tools and one or more Skid Steers to cutdown and consolidate the brush on your property.

Disposal Methods

After the brush has been consolidated, we move on to disposal. Depending on your preference, we can burn or mulch the brush. At this time, we do not offer haul-off.

When such large piles of brush are involved, burning can be dangerous. We'll utilize the Skid Steer and water trailer to manage the size of the fire, and only burn when drought conditions permit us to safely do so.

As for mulching, we'll proceed with a Chipper Shredder or Tub Grinder, and work in conjunction with the Skid Steer and ground personnel to process the material.

About Us

Bad Hog Land Clearing is a small business locally owned and operated out of San Marcos, Texas. Our team is a tight-knit group of dedicated individuals who share a passion for land clearing. We started this outfit with a love for the work, and a desire to bring professional land clearing service to Central Texas.
